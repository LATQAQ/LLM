Warlock邪术师，契术师，王洛克，战锁
Do warlock spells granted by the Expanded
Spell List feature count against the number of spells known? The spells granted
by that feature aren’t automatically known by a
warlock. Those spells are added to the warlock spell list for the character,
who can choose them when learning a new warlock spell of the appropriate level.
Once learned, such a spell does count against the number of spells the warlock
knows.
Q：“扩展法术列表”职业能力获得的法术计入邪术师已知法术吗？
A：邪术师并不会自动习得该能力提供的法术。只有当角色获得新的已知法术时，你可以从该能力提供的法术里选择满足等级条件的加入已知列表。
Does the Eyes of the Rune Keeper invocation
work on magical runes? Eyes of the Rune Keeper lets you read any form of writing,
including the linguistic meaning of a rune, if any.
Q：魔能祈唤“符文守护者之眼”对魔法符文生效吗？
A：“符文守护者之眼”赋予你阅读任何书写的能力，包括符文的语言含义。
If a warlock uses Pact of the Blade to bond
with a magic weapon, does that weapon have to be a melee weapon, and can the
warlock change the weapon’s form? The warlock’s Pact of the Blade feature (PH, 107�C8) lets
you create a melee weapon out of nothing. Whenever you do so, you determine the
weapon’s form, choosing from the melee weapon options
in the Weapons table in the Player’s Handbook (p. 149).
For example, you can create a greataxe, and then use the feature again to
create a javelin, which causes the greataxe to disappear.
You can also use Pact of the Blade to bond
with a magic weapon, turning it into your pact weapon. This magic weapon doesn’t have to be a melee weapon, so you could use the feature on a +1
longbow, for instance. Once the bond is formed, the magic weapon appears
whenever you call your pact weapon to you, and you can’t
change the magic weapon’s form when it appears. For example,
if you bond with a flame tongue (longsword) and send the weapon to an
extradimensional space, the weapon comes back as a longsword when you summon
it. You don’t get to turn it into a club. Similarly, if
you bond with a dagger of venom, you can’t summon it as
a maul; it’s always a dagger.
The feature allows the conjuring forth of a
melee weapon, yet we allow more versatility when it comes to magic weapons. We
didn’t want a narrow focus in this feature to make a
warlock unhappy when a variety of magic weapons appear in a campaign. Does this
versatility extend outside the melee theme of the feature? It sure does, but we’re willing to occasionally bend a design concept if doing so is
likely to increase a player’s happiness.
Q：邪术师在使用“刃之魔契”联结魔法武器时，必须选择近战武器吗？可以改变武器的形态吗？
A：“刃之魔契”能力（玩家手册第107、108页）赋予你凭空创造近战武器的能力。创造的时候你可以从武器表格（玩家手册第149页）的近战武器中选择武器的形态。举例而言，你可以用这个能力创造一把巨斧，然后再次使用它创造一把标枪。创造标枪时，巨斧将消失。
你也可以用“刃之魔契”能力联结一把魔法武器，把它变为你的契约武器。这把武器不必须是近战武器，所以你可以联结一把+1长弓。联结完成后，你召唤契约武器时这把魔法武器就会出现，但你无法改变它的形态。举例而言，如果你联结了一把焰舌长剑并把它转移到异次元空间，那么当它被召唤回来时依然是一把长剑。你无法把它变成一把短棒。同样，如果你联结了一把淬毒匕首，你也无法把它变成一把巨锤。
“刃之魔契”只能用来创造近战武器，但在联结魔法武器时就更灵活一些。在战役里经常会出现各种不同类型的魔法武器，我们不希望因为限制武器类型让邪术师玩家不悦。这种灵活性是否违背了这个能力的近战主题？是的。但我们愿意为了让玩家开心而偶尔在设计上做点变通。
If you’re a Pact of
the Blade warlock, can someone else wield your pact weapon? Yes. Someone other
than the warlock can use a pact weapon before the weapon disappears.
Q：其他人可以使用选择“刃之魔契”的邪术师的契约武器吗？
A：是的。在契约武器消失以前其他人都可以使用它。
Does the warlock’s
Awakened Mind feature allow two-way telepathic communication? The feature is
intended to provide one-way communication. The warlock can use the feature to
speak telepathically to a creature, but the feature doesn’t give that creature the ability to telepathically reply. In
contrast, the telepathy ability that some monsters have (MM, 9) does make
two-way communication possible.
Q：邪术师的“唤醒心灵”能力是双向心灵感应吗？
A：这个能力只提供单向心灵感应。这个能力让邪术师可以通过意念对某个生物说话，但并不能让该生物通过意念回复。作为对比，某些怪物拥有的心灵感应能力（怪物图鉴第9页）是允许双向通信的。
Does using a bonus action break invisibility
from a warlock’s One with Shadows invocation? Taking a
bonus action breaks the invisibility of a warlock’s One
with Shadows. A bonus action is an action.
Q：使用附赠动作会打断邪术师“融身入影”祈唤的隐形效果吗？
A：会。附赠动作也是动作。

Wizard法师
Is an abjurer’s
Arcane Ward healed only when the ward has 0 hit points? The ward regains hit
points whenever the abjurer casts an abjuration spell of 1st level or higher,
not just when the ward has 0 hit points. As is normal for healing, the ward can’t regain more hit points than its hit point maximum: twice the
wizard’s level + the wizard’s
Intelligence modifier.
Q：防护师的“奥术守御”只有在生命值为0时才能恢复生命值吗？
A：任何时候防护师施展1环或更高的防护学派法术都会为“奥术守御”恢复生命值，无论它剩余多少生命值。和普通的治疗一样，“奥术守御”恢复生命值也无法超过其生命值上限，即法师等级的2倍+法师的智力调整值。
Does casting alarm as a ritual heal Arcane
Ward? Any abjuration spell of 1st level or higher cast by an abjurer, including
a ritual, can restore hit points to the abjurer’s
Arcane Ward.
Q：以仪式的方式施展警报术是否能恢复“奥术守御”的生命值？
A：防护师施展的任何1环或更高的防护学派法术，无论是不是仪式，都可以恢复“奥术守御”的生命值。
How does Arcane Ward interact with
temporary hit points and damage resistance that an abjurer might have? An
Arcane Ward is not an extension of the wizard who creates it. It is a magical
effect with its own hit points. Any temporary hit points, immunities, or
resistances that the wizard has don’t apply to the
ward.
The ward takes damage first. Any leftover
damage is taken by the wizard and goes through the following game elements in
order: (1) any relevant damage immunity, (2) any relevant damage resistance,
(3) any temporary hit points, and (4) real hit points.
Q：防护师的“奥术守御”、临时生命、伤害抗力之间如何互相作用？
A：“奥术守御”是一个有生命值的魔法效果，而非创造它的法师的一部分。任何该法师具有的临时生命值、免疫、抗力都与“奥术守御”无关。
“奥术守御”首先承受伤害，剩余伤害按以下顺序判断最终效果：1.相关的伤害免疫；2.相关的伤害抗力；3.临时生命值；4.实际生命值。
An abjurer gains resistance to spell damage
at 14th level. Does the abjurer’s Arcane Ward gain this
resistance when a spell hits the abjurer? The abjurer, not the Arcane Ward,
gains the resistance at 14th level.
Q：防护师在14级时获得对法术伤害的抗力，那么他的“奥术守御”是否也获得对法术伤害的抗力？
A：防护师有，“奥术守御”没有。
Does the evoker’s
Potent Cantrip feature apply to cantrips with attack rolls or only to saves?
Potent Cantrip affects only cantrips that require a saving throw, such as acid
splash and poison spray.
Q：塑能师的“强力戏法”能力只对有豁免的戏法生效，还是对有攻击骰的戏法也有效？
A：“强力戏法”只对有豁免的戏法生效，例如酸液飞溅和毒液喷溅。
Does the 14th-level illusionist ability let
you make something real every round, or just once? Illusory Reality is intended
to make one illusory object real per illusion spell.
Q：幻术师的14级能力是能让你每轮把一个幻象化为真实，还是只能生效一次？
A：“亦真亦幻”能力允许你每次施展幻术系法术时把一个幻象化为真实。
For the Shapechanger feature of the School
of Transmutation, does polymorph need to be prepared if I only want to cast it
without using a spell slot? A transmuter must have polymorph prepared, as
normal, to cast it with the Shapechanger feature.
Q：变化学派的法师在用“变形生物”能力不消耗法术位施展变形术时，是否仍需要准备变形术？
A：变化师仍需要准备变形术以使用“变形生物”能力。

Backgrounds背景
Can you have more than one background? No.
You can have only one background. It establishes key aspects of your life
before you embarked on a life of adventure. If none of the backgrounds
available matches your character concept, talk with your DM and use the
guidelines on page 125 of the Player’s Handbook to
customize your own background.
Q：一名人物可以有多个背景吗？
A：不可以。一名人物只能有一个背景，它确定了你踏上冒险之路前生活的大概情况。如果已有的背景中没有和你角色概念匹配的，你可以和DM沟通，按玩家手册第125页的设计准则自定背景。

Equipment装备
Does heavy armor reduce Dexterity saving throws
or ability checks other than Stealth? If you have proficiency with it, heavy
armor doesn’t affect Dexterity saving throws or ability
checks, other than Stealth.
Q：重甲会降低敏捷豁免或是隐匿之外的属性检定吗？
A：只要你熟练重甲，它就不会影响你的敏捷豁免和隐匿以外的属性检定。
If you attack with a shield―most likely as an improvised weapon―do you
keep the +2 bonus to AC? Attacking with a shield doesn’t
deprive you of the shield’s bonus to AC.
Q：用盾牌（作为临时武器）攻击时，你是否还能保留它提供的+2AC加值？
A：使用盾牌攻击不会让你失去它提供的AC加值。
